# Purpose

## What This Wiki Is

A personal knowledge base — a **second brain** that accumulates and organizes everything I learn, read, and think about. Unlike chat history that disappears, this wiki compounds knowledge over time.

By using an LLM as a "wiki maintainer", this system frees me from administrative overhead and manual bookkeeping. The LLM handles summarizing, cross-referencing, and maintaining the index, while I focus on curation and understanding.

## Scope

This wiki covers all areas of my personal and professional life:

- **Career & Work** — professional skills, workplace knowledge, industry insights
- **Finance & Investing** — personal finance, investment strategies, market analysis
- **Technology & AI** — coding, LLMs, tools, software development
- **Self-Improvement** — goals, habits, health, psychology, productivity
- **Hobbies & Interests** — any deep-dive topic that captures my attention
